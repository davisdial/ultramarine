SALT FLATS

There's an atmosphere at the Salt Flats that you can't really find anywhere else. The emptiness, the plain wind, the white salt meshed with the blue sky provoke a sense of otherwoldliness within you. It's not a place you go to necessarily do anything-rather it's a place you go to simply exist-and maybe find yourself along the way.

OUTSIDE PEACE ON EARTH

Outside Peace On Earth Coffee. It's funny how despite having the worst drinks in town, the shop remains the cornerstone of Provo, Utah. It's evidence of the amount of weight spaces carry. Rustic, chic, almost camp interior design coupled with words of acceptance and love make for a truly special place.

GOODFELLAS

A lucky guy meets a lucky opportunity. 2 albums! Shows! Crowd surfing! Recording studios! It was one of the most thrilling, stressful, wonderful, and confidence building/crumbling expereinces of my life.

CAPS

Me and my two best friends took a spontaneous trip to California for four days. It was as if we nailed the sweet spot in our lives. We all were at the peak of our friendships, we saved up money to spend and enjoy the trip, and we had complete freedom. I haven’t felt freedom like that before, and I haven’t felt it since. That foggy night on the beach, or the urban outfitters dressing room, or playing soccer on that serene field all have this energy that I’ll never forget. Weirdly, this experience is what keeps me going. I keep my head down and continue to work so I can feel this way again.
